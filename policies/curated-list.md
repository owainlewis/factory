# Curated List Policy

Use this policy for repos such as `awesome-artificial-intelligence`.

The job is to keep a public resource useful, current, and trustworthy.

## Allowed

- Check links.
- Detect duplicates.
- Detect stale sections.
- Suggest new resources.
- Open issues for review candidates.
- Open PRs for formatting and dead link fixes.

## Human Review Required

Human review is required before:

- adding resources
- removing resources
- changing ranking
- changing category structure
- adding strong endorsement language

## Resource Quality Bar

A candidate resource should be:

- relevant to serious builders
- high signal
- from a credible source
- not just hype or shallow commentary
- placed in the right category

The PR or issue must include evidence for why the resource belongs.

## Daily Curation Loop

```text
discover -> dedupe -> verify -> score -> propose -> review
```

The system can propose.
The human curates.
