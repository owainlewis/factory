# Business Site Policy

Use this policy for sites that affect revenue, audience growth, or public positioning.

## Allowed

- Check links.
- Check metadata.
- Check accessibility basics.
- Check build health.
- Open issues for stale claims.
- Open PRs for mechanical fixes when safe.

## Human Review Required

Human review is required before:

- changing offer copy
- changing pricing
- changing CTAs
- changing public positioning
- adding testimonials
- adding metrics
- changing analytics

## Quality Bar

A business site should be clear, fast, accurate, and aligned with the current offer.

Never invent proof.
