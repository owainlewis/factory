# Daily Curation Audit

## Schedule

Daily.

## Runner

Codex.

## Goal

Audit the AI resource list and propose useful curation work.

## Steps

1. Read this repo config.
2. Read the repo goal.
3. Inspect the target repo README.
4. Check for broken links.
5. Check for duplicates.
6. Find up to 5 candidate resources from credible sources.
7. Score each candidate for relevance, credibility, practical value, freshness, and fit.
8. Open issues for candidate resources that need human review.
9. Open a PR only for safe mechanical fixes such as dead links or formatting.

## Candidate Evidence

Every candidate issue must include:

- title
- URL
- source
- category
- why it belongs
- score
- what human decision is needed

## Stop Rules

Do not add resources directly.
Do not remove resources directly.
Do not change categories directly.
